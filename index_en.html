Open data of the Bulgarian Parliament
  • List of active Members of Parliament
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/mp-current.xml
  • List of all MPs so far
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/mp-all.xml
  • Detailed data on each MP
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/mp/mp_[unique id].xml
  • Absense sorted by MPs
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/absense-invert.xml
  • Absense sorted by months in one file
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/absense-all.xml
  • Absense sorted by months
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/absense/absense_[year]-[month].xml
  • List of references to transcripts of plenary seetings for the past 20 years
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/plenaryst-all.xml
  • List of references to programs of plenary seetings for the past 10 years
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/plenaryst-p-all.xml
  • List of references to parliamentary control at plenary seetings for the past 10 years
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/plenaryst-c-all.xml
  • Votes for each MP on plenary discussion points and bills - reference list
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/vote-all.xml
  • Votes for each MP on plenary discussion points and bills on a specific date
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/vote/vote_[DDMMYY].xml
  • List of bills - law proposition, laws, desicions and declarations
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/bill-all.xml
  • Detailed data for a proposition or a voted bill
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/bill/bill_[unique id].xml
  • Consultants for MP
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/consultant/consultant_mp.xml
  • Consultants for parliament groups
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/consultant/consultant_pgroup.xml
  • Consultants for parliament committees
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/consultant/consultant_pcommittee.xml
  • List of active parliament groups
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pgroup-all.xml
  • Data on an individual parliament group
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pgroup/pgroup_[unique id].xml
  • List of active parliament committees
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pcomm-all.xml
  • Data on an individual parliament committee
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pcomm/pcomm_[unique id].xml
  • List of all sittings, discussion points, transcripts and reports of all parliament committees
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pcommsit-all.xml
  • List of all sittings, discussion points, transcripts and reports of one parliament committee
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pcommsit/pcommsit_[committee ID].xml
  • Data on each sitting of a parliament committee with details on the discussion points, transcripts, reports and attendance
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pcommsit/pcommsit_[committee ID]_[sitting ID].xml
  • List of all friendship groups with other countries
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pgfriend-all.xml
  • Data on a friendship group
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/pgfriend/pgfriend_[unique id].xml
  • List with public procurements
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/procurement-all.xml
  • Data on a specific public procurement
    http://parliament.yurukov.net/data/procurement/procurement_[уникален–номер].xml
